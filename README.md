# Student Assistant

This project is meant to act as a hub of AI agents that can help improve a college student's workflow.

## Syllabus Slayer

This agent's job is to take a syllabus document and break it down into a timeline of tasks, including preparation tasks.

## Devil's Advocate

This agent's job is to challenge your thesis in order to prepare you for defending your thesis.

## Recursive Researcher

This agent's job is to break a research topic into sub-topics, performing in-depth research on each sub-topic and compiling the information into a single comprehensive summary of information with citations.
